## A) Executive Summary

### What We Are Building
A Spanish-language authority site focused on emergency preparedness kits and scenarios. The site serves Spanish-speaking audiences across Spain, Mexico, and Latin America with practical, non-alarmist guidance for preparing for realistic emergencies: power outages, extreme heat, water disruptions, flooding, and evacuation scenarios.

### Why This Is a Wedge
The Spanish-language emergency preparedness space is dramatically underserved compared to English. Most existing content is:
- Thin listicles with no depth
- Translations of US prepper content (culturally mismatched, fear-based)
- Government PDFs (dry, not actionable)
- No interactive tools exist in Spanish for kit building or calculations

We enter with a differentiated position: **practical tools and scenario-specific checklists** that no competitor offers.

### What Differentiates Us
1. **Interactive Kit Builder**: No Spanish competitor has this. Users configure by scenario, family size, pets, and existing supplies → receive personalized, printable checklist + shopping list.
2. **Calculators**: Water and power calculators with scenario-specific outputs.
3. **Scenario-First Architecture**: We own the long-tail: "qué llevar en un apagón de 3 días", "kit para bebé en evacuación", "agua para ola de calor".
4. **Component Knowledge Base**: Deep pages on each item creating internal linking density.
5. **Printable Outputs**: Every tool produces a clean PDF. This is a link-earning asset.

### How It Monetizes
- **Amazon Affiliates (Primary)**: Tool outputs include optional shopping lists with affiliate links.
- **Future Expansion**: Local affiliate programs (Spain: PC Componentes, El Corte Inglés; Mexico: Mercado Libre).

### How It Scales
- **Language Expansion**: Architecture supports `/es/`, `/en/`, `/pt/` prefixes. Spanish first.
- **Scenario Expansion**: Each new scenario adds a cluster without restructuring.
- **Tool Expansion**: Evacuation route planner, communication plan builder, inventory tracker.

## B) Positioning and Audience

### Primary Audiences

| Segment | Description | Key Scenarios |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Urban Families (Spain)** | Apartment dwellers, 2-4 people | Power outage, heatwave, water cut |
| **Families with Infants** | Parents of children under 3 | Any emergency with infant needs |
| **Pet Owners** | Dogs, cats as family members | Evacuation, shelter-in-place |
| **Car Commuters** | Daily drivers, road trippers | Breakdown, stranded, evacuation |
| **Suburban/Rural (LatAm)** | Houses with more space | Extended outage, flooding, isolation |
| **Elderly Care** | Adult children planning for parents | Medical needs, mobility, communication |

### Scenarios We Cover

**Tier 1 (Launch Priority)**
- Apagón / corte de luz (power outage)
- Ola de calor (heatwave)
- Corte de agua (water outage)
- Evacuación ligera (light evacuation)
- Kit para el coche (car kit)
- Kit 72 horas general

**Tier 2 (Post-Launch)**
- Inundación (flooding)
- Tormenta severa (severe storm)
- Kit para bebé
- Kit para mascotas
- Kit para personas mayores

### Tone of Voice

**Do:**
- Practical, calm, helpful
- "Aquí tienes lo que necesitas saber"
- Specific quantities and reasons
- Use "tú" (informal) for approachability

**Don't:**
- Fear-mongering ("el apocalipsis", "el colapso")
- Prepper culture aesthetics (camo, bunkers)
- Political framing
- Medical advice beyond "include these items"
- Urgency manipulation

### Content Boundaries

| Topic | Allowed | Not Allowed |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| First aid kit items | ✅ List items | ❌ How to treat injuries |
| Water purification | ✅ Include tablets/filter | ❌ Claim specific pathogen removal |
| Food storage | ✅ Shelf life, rotation | ❌ Nutrition advice |
| Self-defense | ❌ Not covered | ❌ Weapons |
| Medical conditions | ✅ "Consult your doctor" | ❌ Specific medical guidance |

## E) Tool Specifications

### 1. Kit Builder (Configurador de Kit)

**Inputs:**
- Scenario: General 72h, Apagón, Ola calor, Corte agua, Evacuación, Coche
- Household: Adults, Children, Infants, Elderly, Pets (dogs by size, cats)
- Duration: 24h, 72h, 7 days, 14 days
- Location: Apartment, House, Car, Combination
- Existing supplies: Checkboxes for items already owned

**Outputs:**
- Personalized checklist grouped by category
- Priority indicators (essential/recommended/optional)
- Shopping list (items not owned)
- Printable PDF
- Optional affiliate links in shopping list

**Quantity Rules:**
| Item | Per Adult/Day | Per Child/Day | Per Infant/Day | Per Dog/Day | Per Cat/Day |
|------|---------------|---------------|----------------|-------------|-------------|
| Water (L) | 3 | 2 | 1.5 | 1 (M) | 0.3 |
| Batteries AA | +4/72h base | — | — | — | — |
| Diapers | — | — | 8 | — | — |

### 2. Water Calculator

**Inputs:**
- People: Adults, Children, Infants
- Pets: Dogs (S/M/L), Cats
- Duration: 24h to 30 days
- Climate: Normal, Heatwave (+50%), High activity (+30%)
- Scope: Drinking only, +Hygiene, +Cooking

**Outputs:**
- Minimum liters
- Recommended liters
- Comfort liters
- Daily breakdown
- Storage options (garrafas, bidones)

### 3. Power Calculator

**Inputs:**
- Devices: Smartphone, Tablet, Laptop, Flashlight, Radio, Fan, CPAP, Custom
- Quantity and charges/hours per day
- Duration: 24h, 72h, 7 days

**Outputs:**
- Total Wh needed
- Category recommendation:
  - <100Wh: Small powerbank
  - 100-300Wh: Large powerbank
  - 300-1000Wh: Portable power station
  - >1000Wh: Large station or generator
- Disclaimer about real-world vs rated capacity
